# Elsevier Official Guidelines

Last checked: 2026-07-06.

Use these official sources first:

- Elsevier author policies and guidelines: https://www.elsevier.com/researcher/author/policies-and-guidelines
- Elsevier artwork and media instructions: https://www.elsevier.com/researcher/author/policies-and-guidelines/artwork-and-media-instructions
- Target journal Guide for Authors: open from the journal home page on Elsevier or ScienceDirect.

## What To Verify Each Time

- Target journal Guide for Authors.
- Article type and word limits.
- Whether highlights are required.
- Whether graphical abstract is required or optional.
- Title page and author details.
- Abstract type, keywords, abbreviations, and main text structure.
- Reference style, reference manager style, and citation examples.
- Figure, table, artwork, permission, and supplementary file requirements.
- Declaration of interest, funding, ethics, consent, author contributions, data availability, and AI-assisted writing disclosure.

## Output Convention

Separate:

- `required by Guide for Authors`.
- `Elsevier general policy`.
- `not confirmed for this journal`.

This prevents generic Elsevier policies from being mistaken for journal-specific submission rules.
