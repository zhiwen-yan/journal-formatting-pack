# Wiley Official Guidelines

Last checked: 2026-07-06.

Use these official sources first:

- Wiley Author Services, prepare a manuscript: https://authorservices.wiley.com/author-resources/Journal-Authors/Prepare/index.html
- Wiley Author Services, data sharing and citation: https://authorservices.wiley.com/author-resources/Journal-Authors/open-access/data-sharing-citation/index.html
- Wiley Online Library journal pages: search the exact journal title plus "author guidelines".

## What To Verify Each Time

Wiley requirements are usually journal-specific. Verify:

- Exact journal Author Guidelines page.
- Article type and word limits.
- Structured or unstructured abstract.
- Title page, anonymization, and blinded review requirements.
- Reference style.
- Figure/table formatting and supporting information.
- Data availability policy.
- Ethics approval, consent, conflicts of interest, funding, author contributions, and acknowledgments.
- Reporting guidelines required by the journal.

## Journal Of Advanced Nursing Note

For Journal of Advanced Nursing or other nursing journals, do not assume the rules from another Wiley journal. Open the journal's current Author Guidelines page from Wiley Online Library and extract article-type rules from that page.

## Output Convention

Label requirements as:

- `confirmed`: found in the current target journal guidelines.
- `publisher general`: found only in Wiley Author Services.
- `needs confirmation`: not found or blocked by access limitations.
