# Frontiers Official Guidelines

Last checked: 2026-07-06.

Use these official sources first:

- General author guidelines: https://www.frontiersin.org/guidelines/author-guidelines
- Frontiers in Pharmacology article types: https://www.frontiersin.org/journals/pharmacology/for-authors/article-types
- Frontiers in Nutrition article types: https://www.frontiersin.org/journals/nutrition/for-authors/article-types

## What To Verify Each Time

- Target journal and specialty section.
- Article type and article-type limits.
- Abstract format and limit.
- Keyword count.
- Main text section order.
- Reference style and citation requirements.
- Figure, table, supplementary material, and data file requirements.
- Required declarations: data availability, ethics, consent, author contributions, funding, conflict of interest, acknowledgments, and generative AI/AI-assisted writing disclosure.

## Initial Checklist

For common original research manuscripts, expect title, abstract, keywords, introduction, materials and methods, results, discussion, and declaration sections. Some article types require different headings or limits.

Frontiers rules are article-type-sensitive. Do not apply an Original Research structure to Review, Brief Research Report, Case Report, Methods, or Protocol manuscripts without checking the current article-type page.

## Missing Information Placeholders

Use these placeholders when details are not supplied:

- `[ethics approval details to be provided by authors]`
- `[informed consent statement to be confirmed]`
- `[data availability statement to be provided]`
- `[author contributions to be completed by authors]`
- `[funding details to be provided or state no funding]`
- `[conflict of interest statement to be confirmed]`
- `[AI-use disclosure to be confirmed]`
