# Oxford University Press Official Guidelines

Last checked: 2026-07-06.

Use these official sources first:

- Oxford Academic authoring journals page: https://academic.oup.com/pages/authoring/journals/preparing_your_manuscript
- Oxford Academic journals home: https://academic.oup.com/journals
- Journal-specific instructions: open the target journal on Oxford Academic and locate "Instructions to authors" or "Author guidelines".

## What To Verify Each Time

- Target journal Instructions to Authors.
- Article type and word limits.
- Abstract and keywords.
- Reference style.
- Figure, table, permissions, and supplementary data rules.
- Data availability, ethics, consent, funding, conflict of interest, author contributions, ORCID, and reporting guideline requirements.

## Output Convention

OUP journals differ by discipline and society partner. Label any publisher-level rule as `general OUP guidance` unless the target journal instructions confirm it.
