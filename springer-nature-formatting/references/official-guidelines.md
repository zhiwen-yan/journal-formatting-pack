# Springer Nature, BMC, And Nature Portfolio Official Guidelines

Last checked: 2026-07-06.

Use these official sources first:

- Springer Nature author resources: https://www.springernature.com/gp/authors
- Springer Nature research data policy information: https://www.springernature.com/gp/authors/research-data-policy
- Springer Nature editorial policies: https://www.springernature.com/gp/editorial-policies
- BMC Public Health preparing your manuscript: https://bmcpublichealth.biomedcentral.com/submission-guidelines/preparing-your-manuscript

## What To Verify Each Time

- Journal family: Springer, BMC, Nature Portfolio, Scientific Reports, or other Springer Nature title.
- Journal-specific submission guidelines.
- Article type.
- Title page requirements.
- Abstract format.
- Main text headings.
- Reference style.
- Figure, table, and supplementary information rules.
- Required declarations and their location.
- Reporting guideline and checklist requirements.

## Common Declaration Areas

Check:

- Ethics approval and consent to participate.
- Consent for publication.
- Competing interests.
- Funding.
- Author contributions.
- Acknowledgments.
- Data availability.
- Code availability if applicable.
- Clinical trial registration if applicable.
- AI-use disclosure if applicable.

## BMC Note

BMC journal instructions often provide a detailed "Preparing your manuscript" page. Use that page for section order and declarations. Do not apply BMC ordering to Nature Portfolio journals unless the target journal says so.
