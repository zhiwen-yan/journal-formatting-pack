# Journal Of Clinical Medicine Official Guidelines

Last checked: 2026-07-06.

Use these sources first:

- Journal of Clinical Medicine instructions: https://www.mdpi.com/journal/jcm/instructions
- MDPI author services: https://www.mdpi.com/authors

## Use Rules

- Treat JCM instructions as higher priority than general MDPI guidance.
- Confirm article type before applying structure.
- Check MDPI statement sections plus any journal-specific wording or placement differences.

## Minimum Review Targets

- manuscript structure
- references
- figures and tables
- Author Contributions
- Funding
- Institutional Review Board Statement
- Informed Consent Statement
- Data Availability Statement
- Conflicts of Interest
