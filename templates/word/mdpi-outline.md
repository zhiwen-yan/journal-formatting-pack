# MDPI Word Structural Draft Notes

This is an unofficial fallback, not an MDPI template file.

- Resolve the exact journal and article type before formatting.
- Use the current official Word template for strict output.
- Let template styles, page settings, headers, footers, and line numbering win over local defaults.
- Label generator output `unofficial structural draft` until it has been migrated into and verified against the official template.
- Keep title, authors, affiliations, correspondence, abstract, keywords, numbered body sections, conditional back matter, and references in the journal's current order.
- Render and inspect every page after any DOCX transformation.

Do not use a fixed Times New Roman 12 pt / 1-inch / 1.5-spacing recipe as a proxy for current MDPI formatting.
